    • Enchanted Kingdom

    Enchanted Kingdom is a beloved theme park located in Santa Rosa, Laguna, Philippines. Covering 25 hectares, it offers a variety of rides and attractions for all ages. The park is managed and operated by Enchanted Kingdom Inc.

    Mount Tagapo

    Mount Tagapo, also known as Susong Dalaga, is the highest point on Talim Island in Laguna de Bay, standing at 438 meters. The hike offers stunning views and is accessible by a ferry ride from Binangonan, followed by a hike through grasslands and bamboo forests. It's popular for its panoramic views and unique location.

    Nuvali

    Nuvali is a 2,500-hectare eco-city in Santa Rosa, Laguna, focused on sustainable living. It features residential areas, business spaces, schools, a hotel, and recreational spots like a multi-functional lake, biking paths, a wildlife sanctuary, and Ayala Malls Solenad.

    Museo de Sta Rosa

    Museo de Sta Rosa in Santa Rosa, Laguna, is a cultural museum showcasing local history and traditions. It's housed in a restored Spanish-era building and is open from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Perfect for history enthusiasts!

    Read More

    The Fun Farm at Sta. Elena

    The Fun Farm at Sta. Elena is a family-friendly destination in Cabuyao, Laguna. It offers a range of outdoor activities including animal interaction, horseback riding, a mini zipline, and spacious play areas. Ideal for a fun day out in nature, it's open Tuesday to Sunday from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
